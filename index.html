Biography

  • Born on October 15, 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu
  • Death Died on July 27, 2015 in Shillong, Meghalaya
  • Occupation Scientist, engineer, author, professor, and politician
  • Served as India's 11th President from 2002 to 2007
  • Education Studied physics and aerospace engineering at the Madras Institute of Technology
  • Contributions Played a leading role in developing India's missile and nuclear weapons programs
  • Family Born into a poor Tamil Muslim family, his father was a boatman and imam, and his mother was a housewife
